About The Club

Cyber Sentinels is the official cybersecurity club of Rajalakshmi Engineering College. Formed by passionate students and guided by expert faculty, the club focuses on building awareness and hands-on skills in ethical hacking, digital forensics, and cyber defense. We conduct internal CTFs, workshops, expert talks, and security awareness campaigns throughout the year.

About REC

Rajalakshmi Engineering College (REC), Thandalam is an autonomous institution affiliated with Anna University and approved by AICTE. Established in 1997, REC is known for its commitment to academic excellence, innovation, and industry-driven learning. REC has consistently ranked among the top engineering colleges in Tamil Nadu with excellent placement records with top MNCs.

Rules & Guidelines

All participants are expected to maintain the highest standards of ethics, fairness, and professionalism throughout the event.

  1. Collaboration is strictly limited to your registered teammates.
  2. Malicious activities, including attacking other teams, the platform, or any external systems, will lead to immediate disqualification.
  3. Sharing flags, answers, or write-ups during the event is not allowed.
  4. Automated tools, brute-force methods, or any kind of unauthorized external assistance are strictly prohibited unless specifically permitted in a challenge.
  5. Any attempt to disrupt, overload, or perform DoS attacks on the platform or infrastructure will result in permanent disqualification.
  6. Teams must have 2 to 3 members and register on Unstop before the deadline.
  7. Only valid submissions in the correct flag format will be accepted.
  8. In the case of a tie, earlier submission time will determine the ranking.
  9. Violations of the rules or unethical conduct at any point will result in disqualification at the organizers’ discretion.
